## Ingredients

### Pie Crust

- 2 1/2 cups (315g) all-purpose flour
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 teaspoon sugar
- 1 cup (225g) unsalted butter, chilled and diced
- 1/4 to 1/2 cup (60-120ml) ice water
